The objectives of the internship

The ojectives of the internship is to discover the how a company works, socialy, economicaly, technicaly and organizationaly.


The internships also allow the student to discover any kind of jobs, from technician in computer science to much more.


The student will be able to gain work experience during his internship and will use the knowledge acquired during his formation.


The work that the student will asked to do will be in related to what he learnt during his formation.The student will therefore be able to do his work.
